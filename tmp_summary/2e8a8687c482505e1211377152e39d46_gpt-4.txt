The speakers are discussing an AI and machine learning specialization that aims to provide solutions for real-world challenges such as public health, climate change, and disaster management. These courses are designed to offer hands-on experience with AI applications to understand how they fit into addressing major global issues. These issues often involve many stakeholders, logistical constraints, and data privacy issues which make the potential solutions complex. The speakers hope that taking this course would inspire students to build, evaluate, and possibly contribute to AI projects that tackle societal problems and make a significant difference.
